What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a 5v5 MOBA created by Riot Games where players choose a variety of champions to destroy the enemy base.

Champions?? What the fuck are those?

Champions are the playable characters in the game. They have a vast variety of archtypes such as Zoe, the Aspect of Twilight, Thresh, the Chain Warden and, (my personal favourite) Teemo, the Swift Scout!

There's over a 100 of them!
